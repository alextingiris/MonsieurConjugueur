Bienvenue à Monsieur Conjugueur

About

Monsieur Conjugueur is a simple, elegant French verb conjugator to help English-speakers that are learning French. Monsieur Conjugueur is completely free to use, and works (and looks great!) on all devices (desktops, tablets, and phones).

Does it work with all verbs?

Monsieur Conjugueur works with all regular -ER, -IR, and -RE verbs, and as of right now, only conjugates in the indicative present tense.
Because this is a free, open-souce project (You download the source code here) , we ask our users to help us build our database of verbs. if you notice that an irregular verb is missing, please let us know. send us the indicitive present tense conjugation and the infinitive form of the verb to alex@tingiris.com, and we will add it to the site ASAP. Thanks for the support!
